About Us

Welcome to Hafna Family Farm

The Hafna Foundation was founded by a bona-fide Village girl who was lucky enough to receive an education. Ms. Fatuma Humairah Mukiibi has spent years privately working hard with meager resources to try and change the lives for the children and the people in her village and her community. Having grown up facing the same problems, she is determined to do her part in partnership with likely minded individuals to provide sustainable life changing services to communities in need .
Hafna is grounded in the belief that access to healthy food , clean water, education and healthy care is a fundamental human right. The Hafna Foundation is committed to bridging gaps in food security, supporting sustainable farming practices and creating opportunities for families to break the cycle of poverty. Whether through growing food in Massachusetts or building water reservoirs in Uganda, we are focused on creating long term postive impacts in the lives of those we serve
